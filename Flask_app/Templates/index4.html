    Here is a brief description of what each trait means

  1. Extraverted: This means I tend to be a little bit more outgoing and socially confident.
  2. Intuitive: This means that I have a good ability of picking up on subtle cues and hidden aspects of interactions or situations.
  3. Feelings: This means that I tend to mostly listen to my emotions when I make important choices.
  4. Judging: This means that I am focused on and dedicated to my goals and rarely get sidetracked.
  5. Turbulent: This means that I am success-driven, perfectionistic, and eager to improve.
